About Us


Along with the development of the economy, the demand for safe and healthy food is being paid more and more attention from consumers. In addition, events such as poor quality food, poisoned fruits, the rise of cancer, etc. have posed an urgent issue for the quality and safety of food hygiene for consumer products. Meeting the increasing demand of consumers for safe agricultural products. Create distribution channels directly from the manufacturer to the final consumer. Promote safe agricultural products and long-term linkage and stable output for safe agricultural producers, contributing to the development of Alberta's agricultural sector. Eversource farm chooses the path of attachment to clean procedures because this is the strength, a favorable direction for the members who are farmers specializing in vegetable cultivation. More than a hectare of land was identified for planting and supplying organic vegetable market under the organic product. The policy is to close the production process so that the vegetables meet organic standards, the Eversource produces vegetable seeds in the foam provided to members and do not use the pesticides. Post-harvest products, the farm semi-processing and fully consuming for customers. Therefore, procedures of the farm are safe and meet organic standard.
