What is Eversource Farm?

Eversource Farm is a newly started Calgary-based ecological farm that covers 154 acres and is located 40 kilometers north of Calgary. It has built four greenhouses involving much more than choosing not to use pesticides, fertilizers, antibiotics, and growth hormones. It has mass production of fresh vegetables such as cucumbers, tomatoes, green peppers, green beans, carobs, pumpkins, carrots, potatoes, garlic and corns. Its mission is to supply Calgarians with the best tasting fresh vegetables as well as affordable prices.
